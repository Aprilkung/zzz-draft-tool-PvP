Recommended Rules

1. This mode is designed for competing in high-score challenges against a single boss in Deadly Assault.

2. If your team's total cost exceeds 350, you will receive an immediate 1,000 point deduction. For every 1 cost point beyond 350, your final score will be reduced by 0.25%, up to a maximum of 60% score reduction.

3. Each team takes turns offering 3 characters to the opposing team. The opposing team selects 1 character from those 3 for the original team to use. After this, each team is free to pick one final character of their choice. Once a character has been picked by any team, it cannot be picked again.
